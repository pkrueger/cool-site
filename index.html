Why Designers are important!

Most of the reason is so sites don't end up looking like this. While the deep purples and popping colors of links seems enticing, it takes the firm hand of a true designer to resist the base urge to use them. We thank you designers. We thank you for your sacrifice. You may not have as much fun as the average joe creating a website that looks like this but at least it will end up being usable.
